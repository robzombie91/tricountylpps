Welcome to Tri-County Letter/Parcel Presort Service, Inc. (TCLPPS). We are a full service mail presort bureau that has been serving the area for over 30 years. Our goal is to provide our customers with a way to save money on their postage, as well as offer our services to ease the sometimes tedious and time-consuming tasks of mail fulfillment.

There have been many changes in the mail system of the United States Postal Service over the years and we do our utmost to keep our customers informed every step of the way. However, it is still a very simple, easy way to find savings in what we, as local businesses, do every day. Our knowledgeable, friendly and helpful staff does its best to identify and inform our customers of the most efficient and effective way to send their mail.

We are committed to our purpose and believe that pennies do add up. We facilitate over a quarter of a million dollars in postage savings per year for our customers. We hope you enjoy being a part of this beneficial service and we look forward to serving you for years to come.
