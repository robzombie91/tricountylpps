Tri-County Letter/Parcel Presort Service, Inc. also offers additional mail fulfillment services. Quite often mailings involve a lot of time consuming tasks that need to be done prior to sending out the mail. We also have automation equipment for many of these services as well. Some of them include:

* folding * USPS rules and regulations assistance
* hand and machine inserting * document and statement printing
* tabbing * optical marking document integrity
* mailing labels * collated and nested inserting
* metering and sealing * NCOA link processing
* merging and purging of mail lists * move update compliance
* ink jet and label addressing * pick up and delivery

 

As always, before any job, please contact us for exact pricing as each job is different. We can also offer advice on mail piece design to make sure that it is automation compatible.
